About Us

Coca-Cola is a carbonated soft drink sold in stores, restaurants and vending machines internationally. It is produced by The Coca-Cola Company of Atlanta, Georgia, and is often referred to simply as Coke. The drink was invented in the late 19th century by John Pemberton and was bought out by businessman Asa Griggs Candler, whose marketing tactics led Coca-Cola to its dominance of the world soft-drink market throughout the 20th century.
